Overview

The Good Artdeco Backend Theme is a Modern and Stunning Backend Theme For Your Odoo V16. This Theme Will Give You A New Experience With Odoo.

Main Highlight Of The Theme are The New Side Bar with Custom Icons, The New Way of navigating with Submenus, The Overlay Layout of The Content Panel (List, Form, Kanban, Calendar, Activity, Pivot and Graph) and The Stylist fonts.

Feel the delightment and the new experience of using Odoo with this stunning, simple and yet familliar and easy to use theme. With this theme you will enjoy staring your screen for hours.
